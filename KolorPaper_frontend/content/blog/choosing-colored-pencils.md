# Introduction

Coloring is one of the most important activities that help children express themselves and develop their creative skills. However, with the wide variety of choices in the market, choosing the right type can be confusing for parents. In this article, we will provide you with a simplified guide to choosing the best coloring tools for your child.

## Colored Pencils

Colored pencils are the perfect choice for beginners because they are easy to use and do not make a mess. When choosing colored pencils for your child, pay attention to the following specifications:

1. **Hardness:** It is preferable to choose colored pencils with a **soft core**, making it easier for the child to color without having to press too hard.
2. **Size:** For young children (under 5 years), thick pencils (**Jumbo**) are best to suit their grip.
3. **Safety:** Make sure the colors are non-toxic and comply with international safety standards.

## Watercolor Pencils

After your child gets used to standard colored pencils, you can introduce watercolor pencils to further develop their skills.

* **Dry Pans:** This is the cleanest and easiest option for children. They only need a brush and a little water.
* **Tubes:** Suitable for older children who are learning how to mix colors to get new shades and master blending.

## One Final Tip

Do not forget to choose high-quality coloring paper to prevent tearing, especially when using water-based colors. You can download many [free coloring pages](/categories) from our website and print them on thick paper for your child to have the best experience.
